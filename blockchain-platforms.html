Blockchain Platforms

This fourth course of the Blockchain specialization provides learners with an understanding of the broader blockchain ecosystem. You'll explore various blockchain platforms, their unique features, consensus mechanisms, and specific industry-wide frameworks.

What You'll Learn

After completing this course, you will be able to:

  • Understand smart contracts as a core idea and computational model of blockchain that enables automation, autonomy, scalability, and transparency.
  • Have an understanding and working knowledge of the emerging blockchain technology landscape beyond Ethereum.
  • Compare and contrast different blockchain platforms (e.g., Bitcoin, Ethereum, Hyperledger Fabric, Polkadot, Solana, etc.) based on their architecture, use cases, and trade-offs.
  • Analyze various consensus mechanisms and their implications.
  • How to design and program smart contracts and decentralized applications considering platform-specific features.
  • How to think of innovative application models, leveraging the unique capabilities of different blockchain technologies.
  • Understand interoperability challenges and solutions between different blockchains.
  • Be aware of current trends and future directions in blockchain platform development.
